ABOUT MAUD

Hello! My name is Maud, and I am a web and graphics designer. I am currently located in Chicago, IL, but I am originally from Michigan. I design and develop websites and web pages, as well as working with branding and print design. I have a BFA in Graphic Design from Central Michigan University. Currently, I work as a Senior Digital Designer for Measured Marketing, specializing in email marketing. I design email ads for clients, and test/code emails for deployment. I also monitor a group of markets and the work passing through them, as well as a junior designer. I assist in training, developing new products and setting up guidelines for them, and interviewing/hiring.
